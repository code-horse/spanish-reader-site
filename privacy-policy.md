## Privacy Policy for Spanish Reader

### 1. Introduction

Welcome to Spanish Reader ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the Spanish Reader mobile application ("Spanish Reader" or "the App") handles your information.

**Developer**: code-horse  
**Contact**: 2008-horse@163.com

### 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Spanish Reader is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- **No account registration required**
- **No analytics or tracking SDKs**
- **No advertising**
- **No personal profile collection**

### 3. Purchase & Subscription Information

The App uses different purchase models depending on platform:

- **iOS**: one-time paid download from the App Store
- **Android**: free download with optional Pro subscription (managed by RevenueCat + Google Play)

For Android subscriptions, RevenueCat processes anonymous purchase-related data (such as anonymous app user ID and entitlement state) to verify access. No name, email, phone number, or other direct personal identifiers are sent by the App.

- RevenueCat Privacy Policy: [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- Google Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Spanish Reader stores user data locally on your device:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Browsing history | Revisit pages | Local device storage |
| Bookmarks | Save pages | Local device storage |
| Search history | Faster search reuse | Local device storage |
| Saved vocabulary/sentences | Learning review | Local device storage |
| Personal notes | Study annotations | Local device storage |
| App settings | Preferences (theme, accent, TTS speed, etc.) | Local device storage |
| Temporary recordings | Pronunciation practice | Local device storage (temporary) |
| Entitlement cache (Android) | Pro access status | Local secure/local storage |

All above data stays on device unless explicitly sent by a feature you trigger (for example translation).

### 5. Network Usage

The App uses network access for:

- Loading websites in the built-in browser
- Optional translation requests (Bing/Google) when you tap translate
- Android subscription verification and purchase flow (RevenueCat/Google Play)

Spanish Reader does not run a first-party backend for storing your personal learning data.

### 6. Third-Party Services

#### Translation (Optional)

When you initiate translation, selected article text is sent to your chosen provider:

- Bing Translate (`www.bing.com`, `cn.bing.com`)
- Google Translate (`translate.googleapis.com`)

Only the text to be translated and language parameters are sent. We do not upload your local archive, notes, bookmarks, or account profile data.

#### Platform WebView

Web pages viewed through the App are controlled by those third-party websites and their own privacy policies.

### 7. Microphone & Speech Recognition

Spanish Reader requests microphone and speech recognition permissions only for speaking/pronunciation features.

- Audio is used for local speaking practice and scoring workflows.
- The App is configured to prefer on-device recognition where available.

### 8. Children's Privacy

Spanish Reader does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since we do not require account registration or collect profile data, the App can be used without submitting personal identity information.

### 9. Data Deletion

You can delete local data from app settings:

- **Clear Data**: browsing/bookmarks/search history
- **Clear Archive**: saved words, saved sentences, notes

You can also uninstall the App to remove all local application data.

### 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
