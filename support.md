# Spanish Reader - Support

## About Spanish Reader

Spanish Reader is a mobile app that helps you learn Spanish pronunciation and reading with annotation, listening, and speaking-practice tools.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Do I need an account?**  
A: No. Spanish Reader does not require account registration.

**Q: Is there advertising in the app?**  
A: No. Spanish Reader is ad-free.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: Your learning data is stored locally on your device.

### Reading & Pronunciation

**Q: How do I show or hide pronunciation annotations?**  
A: Use the pronunciation toggle in the toolbar/settings.

**Q: Can I switch accents?**  
A: Yes. You can switch between Spain and Latin America accent preferences in settings.

**Q: Why does reader mode fail on some pages?**  
A: Some pages have dynamic/complex layouts that are difficult to parse into a clean reading view.

### Listening & Speaking Practice

**Q: How do I improve TTS quality?**  
A: Install higher-quality Spanish voices in your device system TTS settings.

**Q: Why can't the app hear me?**  
A: Check that microphone and speech-recognition permissions are enabled in system settings.

**Q: Is pronunciation scoring always exact?**  
A: Scoring is a learning aid and can be affected by microphone quality, background noise, and speech recognition behavior.

### Translation

**Q: Which translation engines are supported?**  
A: Bing Translate and Google Translate.

**Q: Is translation automatic?**  
A: No. Translation runs only when you explicitly trigger it.

### Data Management

**Q: How can I delete my data?**  
A: Use in-app settings:

- **Clear Data** for browsing/bookmark/search data
- **Clear Archive** for saved words/sentences/notes

You can also uninstall the app to remove local data.
